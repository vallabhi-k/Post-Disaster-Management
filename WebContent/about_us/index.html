Welcome to SOFT-PAW! We are an advertising organisation.The site has been live for the past 3 years now and we have received overwhelming response from our lovely pet-parents and pet-buddies. Thank you for stopping by!
SOFT PAW is not only a commercial organisation, but we really do care for all the animals.Time and again SOFT PAW has always tried to step up for helping stray and injured animal. The SOFT PAW pet vehicle is one such attempt to help these animals.The vehicles goes on round atleast once a week. Our lovely volunteers help us make this possible.A team of veterinary doctors and few social-workers patrol around in this van to help the needy.We are thankful to them for their contribution.
We are proud to be awarded by 'PetsForAll' Magazine as "Best Commercial Organization working for stray animals' care".
